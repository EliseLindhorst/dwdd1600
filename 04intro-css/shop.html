Silk Sonic's Official Album

An Evening with Silk Sonic is the debut studio album by American musical superduo Silk Sonic, composed of Bruno Mars and Anderson .Paak. It was released on November 12, 2021, by Aftermath Entertainment and Atlantic Records. We have the standard edition of the album or the delux addition with the added bonus track "Love's Train".
