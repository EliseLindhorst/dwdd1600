Bruno Mars

Peter Gene Hernandez, known professionally as Bruno Mars, is an American singer-songwriter and record producer. He is known for his stage performances, retro showmanship, and for performing in a wide range of musical styles, including pop, R and B, funk, soul, reggae, disco, and rock. Bruno Mars performances with Silk Sonic at Dolby Live will be a phone-free experience.

Anderson .Paak

Brandon Paak Anderson, better known by his stage name Anderson .Paak, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and drummer. He released his debut mixtape, O.B.E. The 2024 Residency Tour

Silk Sonic is proud to annouce their residency tour with the following guests

  1. Bootsy Collins
  2. Thundercat
  3. Erykah Badu

We are beyon excited to be able to be holding this residency tour at Dolby Live at Park MGM in Las Vegas, Nevada. Dolby Live is an indoor amphitheater on the grounds of the Park MGM casino hotel in Paradise, Nevada. Opening in December 2016, the theater primarily hosts concerts and residencies and is the second-largest theater on the Las Vegas Strip. The theater sits adjacent to the T-Mobile Arena and Toshiba Plaza.
