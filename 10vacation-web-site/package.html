Packages

Welcome to Guardians of the Galaxy Tours! Learn more about us and some of the packages you can choose for your tour.

Vol 1 Package

Set sail with our Vol. 1 Package which takes you to two of the most iconic planets from the first Guardians of the Galaxy movie. Spend the first 3 days flying around and looking at the sights of Xandar, home to the Nova Empire and a population of twelve billion. You will get to have a one on one meet and greet with Nova Prime Irani Rael who will tell you all about the rich history of the planet including the battle of Xandar. The next location will be Knowhere which has been reconstructed due to the latest battle with the High Evolutionary. There you will get to see the busy city life and meet many different alien races.

The Vol. 1 Package costs 1,500 per person which includes lodging and transportation.

Vol 2 Package

With our Vol. 2 Package you will be able to travel to two more planets that were featured in the second Guardians of the Galaxy film. The first three days you will get to travel to Sovereign and see the beautiful golden city that was made by the High Evolutionary. The second planet you will get to see is a replica of Ego, the living planet. After defeating his father Peter Quill was insipired by the beauty of the world his father made that he did his best to find and make an exact replica of this wondeful planet. With it's vibrant colors and striking visuals Vol. 2 is the perfect choice for those who love eye catching locations.

The Vol. 2 Package costs 1,500 per person which includes lodging and transportation.

Vol 3 Package

The Vol 3. package is an amazing choice. You will be given the chance to stay at 2 planets that have been hand selected from the last Guardians of the Galaxy movie. The first planet you will get to visit is Orgoscope where you will get to see how the High Evolutionary was able to do his experiments for such a long time before the Guardians finally took him down. The second planet you will be able to visit is Counter-Earth. You will get to walk around in a world very similar to ours but populated with Humanimals. This package is perfect for the long time fan who wants to see and learn about the history of Rocket Raccoon.

The Vol. 3 Package costs 1,500 per person which includes lodging and transportation.

Video Game Package

The last package is the Video Game Package which will let you journey to 3 different planets in the span of 6 days. The first planet is Lamentis, you will get to see Mantis' homeplanet and learn about how she got her powers. The second planet you will visit is Seknarf Nine, there you will get to see the Queen of Monsters and her wide collection of creatures. The last planet will be Maklu IV, the birthplace of Drax the destroyer. These three planets let you see many ecosystems and the more natural side of the galaxy. This package is great for people who want to see more planets in our universe.

The Video Game Package costs 1,750 per person which includes lodging and transportation.
