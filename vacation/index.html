Explore the Galaxy with the Guardians

Join the Guardians of the Galaxy in an amazing tour around some of planets they have been to and fought at. The Guardians of the galaxy are a team of intergalactic mercenaries who have fought around the universe. They have also teamed up with Avengers and helped defeat Thanos. Their many adventures and battles have made them a force to be reckoned with and makes them an amazing crew for your out of this world experience.

The Milano

The first ship we offer is the Milano which was used during the Battle of Xandar. The Milano was first gifted to Peter Quill, Star Lord, when he was 10 years old. It was made by the Ravagers and then brought back to life after the battle by the Nova Corps. The Milano is named after Captain Star Lord's childhood crush Alyssa Milano. Along with Captain's quarters there are two sleeping bunks located on this ship. The Milano is mainly used for smaller and VIP tours of the galaxy.

The Bowie

The Guardian's new spacecraft is significantly larger than another other ships they have had in the past. The Bowie is now the Guardian's primary ship used for larger trips.This ship is named after the famous singer David Bowie. The Guardians have a tradition of naming all their spacecrafts after singers. Another one of their ships is named the Benatar after Pat Benatar. This ship has the ability to become invisible and had three detachable pods. The bowie is currently being owned and operated by the new captain of the Guardians Rocket Raccoon.

The Legendary Star Lord
Peter Jason Quill (also known as Star Lord) is one of the two captains you can request for your tour. He is a Celestial-Human hybrid who was taken from Earth in 1988 and was raised by Yondu. He started in the Ravagers before forming the Guardians of the Galaxy with the origian members Groot, Drax, Rocket, and Gamora. If you want an amazing tour around the galaxy with an amazing soundtrack to go along with it, Peter is the captain for you.

Rocket Raccoon
Subject 89P13 or more formerly knows as Rocket Raccoon is a Raccoon who was experimented on by the High Evolutionary. After years of fighting with the Guardians of the Galaxy Rocket is now one of the captains you can hire for your tour around the galaxy. Rocket has genius-level intellect and is a master engineer and tactician. He has amazing experience on the field and can fly a ship like no other creature can! If you're looking for a tour that informs you about everything down to the last detail, Rocket may be the perfect captain for you.
